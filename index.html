Importance of Meals

Breakfast

Breakfast is the most important meal of the day. It gives you energy to start your day and improves concentration.

Lunch

Lunch keeps your metabolism active and helps maintain energy levels through the afternoon.

Dinner

Dinner should be light and balanced. It helps your body repair and prepare for the next day.
